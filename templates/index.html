Summarization Methods

AI Summarization (BART)

Our advanced method using Facebook's BART (Bidirectional and Auto-Regressive Transformers) model, capable of generating concise, human-like summaries by understanding and rephrasing the content.

How BART Works

  1. Text Chunking: Intelligently splits long articles into manageable pieces (512 tokens each)
  2. Neural Processing: Uses advanced AI to understand context, relationships, and key concepts
  3. Smart Generation: Creates new, coherent sentences that capture essential information
  4. Seamless Integration: Combines chunk summaries into a flowing, natural narrative

Extractive Summarization (TextRank)

A reliable method that selects and arranges the most important sentences from the original text, ensuring 100% factual accuracy by preserving the author's exact words.

How TextRank Works

  1. Content Extraction: Uses advanced tools to extract clean article content
  2. Sentence Analysis: Breaks text into sentences and identifies key terms
  3. Importance Mapping: Creates a network of sentences connected by shared concepts
  4. Smart Ranking: Uses Google's PageRank algorithm to identify vital sentences
  5. Natural Flow: Preserves original order of selected sentences for readability
