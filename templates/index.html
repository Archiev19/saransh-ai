AI Summarization Methodology

Summarization Approach

Saransh AI uses an extractive summarization approach based on the TextRank algorithm. Unlike abstractive summarization (which can hallucinate content), extractive summarization selects and arranges the most important sentences from the original text, ensuring factual accuracy.

How TextRank Works

  1. Content Extraction: We use Newspaper3k library to extract the main content from web pages, with a fallback to BeautifulSoup for sites with complex structures.
  2. Sentence Tokenization: The extracted text is split into individual sentences for analysis.
  3. Similarity Graph: The algorithm creates a graph where sentences are nodes, and the edges represent similarity between sentences (based on word overlap).
  4. PageRank Algorithm: Similar to how Google ranks web pages, TextRank identifies the most central (important) sentences in the document.
  5. Sentence Selection: The top-ranked sentences are selected (about 15% of the original content) and arranged in their original order to maintain context and flow.
  6. Final Formatting: The extracted sentences are organized into paragraphs for improved readability.

Key Benefits

  • Factual Accuracy: Uses only sentences from the original text, eliminating hallucinations
  • Contextual Understanding: Selects sentences based on their importance to the overall document
  • Content Preservation: Maintains the author's original phrasing and terminology
  • Fast Processing: Works efficiently without requiring large language models
